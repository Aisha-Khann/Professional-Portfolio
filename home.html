Introduction

Welcome to my page! My name is Aisha, and I am a first year student currently studying Computer Science at the University of Guelph in Guelph ON, Canada. This page serves as a portfolio to get you to learn about me, my interests, background skills, career path and future goals.

I grew up in a small town in the Greater Toronto Area, Milton Ontario. This is where I graduated both elementary school and highschool. The highschool I went to was St. Francis Xavier Catholic Secondary, where I met many of my long term and current close friends. I am super close with my younger sister, Amna, and I have an older brother as well who's finishing up his degree in Electrical Engineering at Toronto Metropolitan University. I live with my two parents, my father works in Windsor, Ontario as a Mechanical Engineer and my mother works nearby in Quality Assurance. I'm super close with my family, and they've inspired me to become the hard-working individual I am today.

When I first moved to Milton at the age of 8, most areas were under construction and there were not too many elementary schools and highschools, as well as there being only one mall, Walmart and Cineplex. As the years went by, Milton began expanding quite rapidly, and is not considered a town anymore, moreso a small city. Growing up in a small suburban town really spiked me to gain interest in the bigger city and what large companies and corporte jobs had to offer, as it was different from what I would usually see in my day to day life.

Once I attained my license at 16, I started exploring other cities more often with friends and family. This influenced my interests in going out to discover new restauraunts, malls and outdoor activities. My hobbies and interests include spending time with friends and family, baking, artistry, shopping, makeup and beauty. I did not grow up playing too many sports, I did play soccer and badminton in elementary though. I love visiting Mississauga and Toronto to explore new food spots with my friends, as well as visit bigger shopping malls. On a day-to-day basis, my friends and I love driving around, grabbing coffee and going out to enjoy activities together. Summer is my favourite season, not just because my birthday falls in June, but I love spending time in the sun, enjoying sunsets, going for walks and longdrives, and enjoying cold-drinks, bonfires and barbeques.
